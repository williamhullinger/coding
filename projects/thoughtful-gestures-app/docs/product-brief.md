# Product Brief — Thoughtful Gesture App

## One-liner
A mobile-first app that helps you send the right thoughtful message + card in seconds, and reminds you before important dates.

## Target users
- Busy adults who want to be thoughtful but forget
- People who struggle with “what to say”
- Partners/family members who want reliable reminders

## Primary user stories
- As a user, I can pick a card fast by category.
- As a user, I can personalize it with a name + message.
- As a user, I can save favorites and reuse them quickly.
- As a user, I can save people + dates so I don’t forget.
