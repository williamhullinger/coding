Vendor Participation Policy

Markets are monitored at check-in only, and we do not restrict the number of vendors who may participate.

There may be multiple vendors in the same category, and we welcome everyone to attend.

Note: This policy does not apply to special events. Acceptance is required for the following:

  • Independence Day Celebration
  • Farm and Harvest Festival
  • Christmas Market

What to Expect

The Rogersville Pop-Up Market is a growing outdoor market featuring local artisans, makers, and small businesses. Come support local, discover new favorites, and enjoy a fun community atmosphere.
